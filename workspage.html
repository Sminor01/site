THE WORK

Currently, as an Executive Mixed Reality Producer for Entertainment Experiences for Meta's Reality Labs, I'm working with an incredible team to develop the next generation of mixed reality entertainment experiences.

As Creative Director and Co-Lead for Tupa Labs' Bored Ape Yacht Club from 2023-2024, I was charged with amplifying the brand, expanding its reach, cementing its place in pop culture, and empowering its vibrant community of builders.

From 2020-2023, I drove creative and strategy for WarpSound (formerly known as Warp Records) as Executive Creative Director, pushing the boundaries of adaptive AI music, virtual artistry, and interactive music experiences.

From 2017-2021, I oversaw creative and production in multiple VP Creative roles at Live Nation across a wide array of multi-platform sponsorship and integrated marketing projects, as well as a variety of innovative, emerging media, broadcast, and extended reality (XR) content initiatives.

Before Live Nation, I worked as CEO and Executive Creative Director across all of the design, branding, interactive, experiential, and content projects that came out of The Wylding Creative - the top-tier creative agency I re-founded in 2009 and grew into an award-winning integrated creative and production company with a studio location and $10M+ in annual gross revenue.

Over the years, I've also had the pleasure of Executive Producing, Creative Directing, and Directing a wide array of award-winning music videos, commercials, branded content, and immersive experiences with a myriad of production companies, agencies, and some of the most culturally influential artists and brands in the world.
